A month after he was rescued from a water perpetual cat factory and taken to the hospital.

Registration Date 2020-06-25 12:59:33 PM Author Life Attachment activitydata_14_02

When the hospital life of the 8 dogs rescued from the Busan Water Permanent Cat Factory was almost a month old,

S4 of them had been discharged and admitted to the Life office.

The renovation of an office space for cats who would be admitted after discharge lasted for several days.

The other 4 had to be treated further in the hospital and were subsequently discharged and admitted to the Life office.

How did the appearance of cats after a month of treatment change?

Xia found very pretty eyes,
White looked like a completely different cat.

Cats that should have been treated and cared for in this way had been used and sacrificed, reduced to a tool of profit.

Life will take the lead. We will create a society that respects the life of animals.

With the exception of these 8, the rest of the cats are still used as tools for the production industry.

The municipality has put a shield of "corrective orders" in front of it, and cats, which were illegal tools, are legitimate tools

You just have to neglect what is being washed.

We will talk about the passive administration of Busan City and the Swimming District Office again at a later date.

Life tells us that we will not give up and will work to the end to rescue the rest of the cats.

Let me introduce you to the changed appearance of cats.

1. Xia before 490g, after 990g

2. Ferry before 310g, after 790g

Dongle before 1.6kg, after 1.91kg

4. Curl before 2.3kg, after 2.51kg

5. Cheese before 2.9kg, after 3.27kg

6. Gray before 2.2kg, after 2.65kg

7. mugbefore 2.5kg, after 2.78k

8. White before 2.8kg, after 3.19kg

My appearance and facial expression changed so much that I felt as if I was looking at other cats.

This wasn't the time to fully regain health yet, but it did change a lot compared to when it was rescued.

Isn't it time to change this society that carelessly treats and uses animals to make a profit?

The Government, the National Assembly, and the municipalities should do what they can, such as overhauling the relevant laws and changing the standards of administrative processing.

Strengthening licensing standards for the animal production industry,
Strengthening the punishment of unlicensed production,
Introduction of animal production industry history system, etc.

The government and the National Assembly will have to come up with measures to prevent life from being used merely as a tool of profit.
