[Press Release] Proposed Amendment of Part of the Animal Protection Act

Registration Date 2020-08-19 4:41:22 PM Author Life Attachment Notice_and_press4_02.jpg

Our animal protection organization Life has been working with the Suiper-Permanent Unlicensed Cat Factory in Busan City in February
In May, Kim Hashi Dae-dong-myeon caught and rescued an unlicensed cat factory.
There has been a constant shout out about the problems of the unlicensed animal production industry.

Based on this experience, the Democratic Party's Choi In-ho (National Assembly's Committee on Agriculture, Forestry, Livestock, Food and Ocean Fisheries) in the Congressional Office
Presented the necessity and direction for this amendment,
Congressman Choi In-ho's office promised to continue to improve the institutional deficiencies together.

If there is no awareness of the underlying problem with the act of buying and selling animals,
I don't think we can solve the problem of animal cruelty or organic animals.
