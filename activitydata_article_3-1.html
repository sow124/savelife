[Activity Report] Cat rescue activities trapped in the West East Drainage Pipe

Registration Date 2022-03-01 1:53:20 PM Author Life Attachment activitydata_03_01

A tip came in from a tea road in the western east of Busan that there was a kitten trapped ina drainage pipe.

The kitten was trapped for close to ten days, so
rescue was urgent.

When we arrived at the scene and looked at it,
the drainage pipe
where the kitten was trapped was connected to the elementary school next door.

However, when we tried to rescue, it was over the weekend and it
was difficult to find help from those in
need of rescue.

It was
also difficult to determine where the kitten had entered
the drain.

Nevertheless, many people have helped with the rescue.
I was able to find a repair technician to dismantle the plumbing.

But!
Dismantling the drain pipe and seeing that the cat that was trapped
It was not a kitten, but a large adult cat.

The rescue items that have been prepared are
They were all suitable for young cats,

so even if they couldn't take them to the hospital after some trouble.
I decided to find a way to live and let it go.

The rescue team, who had since watched the cat return, breathed a sigh of relief.

Thanks again to the Kumjung District Office, the elementary school officials, and
many others who helped with the rescue.

If you see an animal in need in the future, please feel free to contact us.
Life will also work harder for the rights and protection of animals.
