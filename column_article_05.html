Pet Healthcare A to Z in Winter

Registration Date 2020-12-07 3:09:19 PM Author Life Attachment column_img05.jpg

In December, the weather got a lot colder.
Just as many people do not adapt to the cold, complaining of cold body aches and feelings of helplessness.
Dogs and cats also tend to develop diseases frequently due to severe sun crossings and winter cold.
Recognize the diseases that occur in dogs and cats during the holiday season and in the winter months.
Introduce simple health care methods that you can take care of at home.

◆Cold during the ring season
Causes: severe sun crossing, lack of sunlight, dryness of the room due to heating, decreased walking and exercise,
Symptoms: cough, runny nose, dry cough with a prickly throat, loss of appetite
Home care: Maintain body temperature, increase humidity (wet towels, humidifiers), nutritious meals
When to visit the veterinary clinic: When you are having a hard time coughing over and over again, when you are unable to sleep well because of coughing,
When anorexia persists for more than a day, when the respiratory rate persists more than 60 times per minute

◆Diarrhea during the ring season
Causes: severe crossing, decreased immunity, excessive snack feeding (jerky, dog gum, etc.), viral enteritis
Symptoms: vomiting, diarrhea, loss of appetite, abdominal pain
Home care: Warmth, back and belly massage, easy-to-digest nutrient flow diet,
Timing of veterinary visits: vomiting more than 3 times, repeated diarrhea more than 4 times, black or red diarrhea,
When anorexia persists for more than a day, when you feel abdominal pain and moan

Tips for Pet Health Care in Winter!
