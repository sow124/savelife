[Animal Protection Act Amendment]
The amendment to the Animal Protection Act has been passed in the plenary session of the National Assembly.

Registration Date 2022-05-11 4:35:32 PM Author Life Attachment Notice_and_press1_01.jpg

[Animal Protection Act Amendment] Amendments to the Animal Protection Act
On April 5, it passed the plenary session of the National Assembly.

54 amendments to the law were incorporated.
The amendment to the Animal Protection Act, which was passed as an alternative to the chairperson of the Agriculture, Sea,
> Reinforcement of penalties for illegal animal production establishments
It includes contents such as.

From a fine of not more than 5 million won
> Imprisonment for not more than 2 years or a fine of not more than 20 million won for unlicensed business
> Imprisonment for not more than 1 year or a fine not exceeding 10 million won for unregistered business

Reinforcement of penalties for illegal animal production establishments
In 2020, with Life's proposal
Choi In-ho, a member of the Democratic Party of Korea, initiated the initiative.
It is also an amendment to the law.

Life is the last year of 2020,
<Busan Suyeong-gu Cat Factory> as caught and rescued.

The mother and son who ran the place
It has illegally raised, breeded, and sold 250 cats.
It was an unlicensed animal production plant.

In addition, in 2020, those caught by Life
<Cat Factory, Daedong-myeon, Gimhae, Gyeongnam >
Likewise, it was an unlicensed animal production plant.

In the past, unlicensed animal producers
Even if caught, only a fine was imposed, but
Due to the passage of this bill
The punishment could be up to imprisonment.

Thank you for your efforts to amend the Animal Protection Act.
I would like to thank In-Ho Choi, Member of the Democratic Party of Korea.
