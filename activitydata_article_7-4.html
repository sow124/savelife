Registration Date 2021-05-14 6:14:23 PM Author Life Attachment activitydata_07_04

We will inform you of the progress of the treatment of the bean-tooth and the status of the fundraising.

Gyeonggi-do, Mars, transported Bin, a quadriplegic dog found by an informant, to a cooperative hospital for an MRI The result is shown below.

Bin-Yi, who was being treated according to MRI findings, suddenly developed symptoms such as peritonitis, pancreatitis, and hepatic failure.

The medical staff did their best to treat them, but at one time they were in a serious state of distress.

As a result of the best treatment of the medical team, starting today, Vinh has stopped treatment for peritonitis and pancreatitis, with a focus on the rehabilitation of myelopathy.
