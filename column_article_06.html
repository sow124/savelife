Dog Adoption Eligibility Test

Registration Date 2020-12-07 3:09:19 PM Author Life Attachment column_img06.jpg

As pets spread to the mood of the times, many people consult with puppy adoption.
As much as the comfort your dog brings
The responsibilities and obligations to care for your dog's life are greater than you can imagine.
As a companion in the position of trying to adopt a puppy
I encourage you to test how qualified you are.

▶Dog (Puppy) Adoption Eligibility Test

1. Which breeds are suitable for growing in an apartment (shared house)?
2. Which breeds do not fall out well?
3. Which breeds do not bark well?
4. Which varieties need a large outdoor space?
5. What are the preparations for adopting a dog?

If you know more than 4 of the five questions, you're well qualified to adopt a dog.
The answer is below.

Through a column by Park Soon-suk, a veterinarian who serves as Life's advisor.
