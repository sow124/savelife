[Please join us.] Kim Hashi Illegal Cat Factory 1st Trial Judgment Appeal Opinion Submitted (+Delivered Completed)

Registration Date 2021-01-28 12:25:23 PM Author Life Attachment Notice_and_press3_04.jpg

There was a 1-trial sentence for a cat factory owner in Daedong-myeon
, Kim Hashi, which the animal protection group Life caught in May last year.

The employer was found guilty of all offenses punishable by imprisonment of not more than two years or a fine of not more than 20 million won for violating the Animal Protection Act (animal cruelty), a fine of not more than 5 million won (for the production of unlicensed animals), imprisonment of not more than two years for violation of the Veterinarian Act, or
a fine of not more than 20 million won.

Nevertheless, the 1st Trial Court only sentenced the owner to 4 months in prison, 1 year
of probation and a fine of 3 million won.

The treatment of 29 animals rescued by the Life,

and Life has not yet repaid the cost of this treatment.

However, the owner has taken unfair advantage by abusing the animal, and
even if such an act is discovered, he will be exempt for a fine of only 3 million won.
