Gyeonggi-do Unauthorized Dog Breeding Grounds 1st Rescue

Registration Date 2020-11-13 04:47:56 PM Author Life Attachment activitydata_11_03

The first rescue at the 110 unlicensed breeding grounds in Gyeonggi Province is underway.

Most of the 110 dogs are in need of veterinary treatment,
First of all, 18 dogs in the 1st round with Seoul
We are distributing and transporting them to the veterinary hospital in Gyeonggi-do.

The rest of the animals in need of treatment are also provided through the 2nd Rescue
We hope to get you to the hospital quickly.

We will create a world where animals and people coexist.
