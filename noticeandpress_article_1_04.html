Suyeong-gu Suyeong-gu cats sentenced to probation for operating an illegal breeding ground for 250 cats

Registration Date 2021-10-28 03:16:00 PMAuthor Life Attachment Notice_and_press1_04.jpg

In March 2020, Life discovered a cat factory located in a residential area of Busan City.

It had 250 cats locked up and bred,
Allegations of animal cruelty have been acknowledged, including untreated treatment for diseases such as skin and respiratory diseases in an unsanitary space.

They even use disposable syringes to administer vaccine drugs, etc.
He has also committed acts that amount to the current Veterinarian Act as an unlicensed practice.

However, for them, the court sentenced them to only 6 months in prison, 2 years of probation, and a fine of 2 million won each.

Life was incurred, with tens of millions of dollars incurred in the treatment of animals rescued from the cat factory.
We are still paying back the hospital bills.

However, the cat factory owner abused the animals and took advantage of tens of millions of won in ill-gotten a profit.
It is unfortunate that if you pay a fine of only 2 million won, you will be exempted.

In addition, at the time of the cat factory discovery, the swimming district and Busan City
I think the passive administration, which has quarantined only 10 cats, deserves to be condemned.

The ruling will also have to look back at whether the court's lukewarm judgment encourages animal cruelty.
