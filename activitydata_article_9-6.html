[Gimpo City Illegal Dog Kennel] I would like to thank the many people who have extended their helping hands.

Registration Date 2021-01-11 04:15:08 PM Author Life Attachment activitydata_09_06 <Thank you>

On 23 October, Life was in Gimpo
During animal rescue operations
What a ridiculous environment
Found a dog kennel.

As the representative leading the organization,
As an animal rights activist
Over the past few years
We have been exposed to numerous animal cruelty sites.

But, more than any animal cruelty scene
I was shocked by the more harrowing appearance.

Life is a fledgling animal rights organization that has just been born.
Cat factory detection and rescue in February and May, and
Protection due to activity
I was in a very difficult situation.

Hydrostatic Cat Factory

In this situation, there are more than 100 animals,
In addition, 100 of them have a disease condition
Rescuing dogs means
It was a wall of reality that I would never dare to imagine.

My worries only went deeper.
The deeper the worry, the more it stands in the way of the walls of reality.
He was resentful.

Quickly began the rescue
Even the existence of the organization
We could have fallen into a dangerous situation.

but
With your eyes open, you are in a situation where you can't possibly look at it.
I couldn't just ignore the dogs as if they didn't know.

This is because I've been an animal rights activist.
Encourage citizens to protect animals.
It was contrary to what he shouted.

Until you decide to make up your mind to rescue
The time it took wasn't long.

Let's consider the bankruptcy of the group in the worst case,
Irresponsible, but through this commitment,
My heart became lighter.

Life Alone in this ugly, huge dog kennel
It was hard to deal with.
Life has turned to several agencies and organizations for help.

Dogs that are almost diseased.
Up to 100 dogs
There was no room to protect them all at once.

However, thank you so much
More and more institutions have joined Life's proposal,
In
consultation with the relevant authorities, animals whose disease appears to be serious
First of all, rescue and transport them to the hospital.
The rescue began on November 13.

The structure of the animals is
November 13-December 7
It was conducted 7 times.
This saved 93 dogs.

The rescued dogs are in cooperative hospitals across the country.
I was transported and treated and cared for.

The full word of this Gimpo illegal dog kennel is
Through SBS YouTube Animal Look Channel
It is being made public.

What used to be thought of as a simple illegal breeding ground
The more you dig, the more you could hardly have imagined it.
Things are happening.

Updated Animal Look
Please keep interested.

And, in this formidable rescue project,
There are many who have offered a helping hand.

If it weren't for their dedicated efforts,
I probably couldn't get a result like this today.

It's late but I rented this place
Bow your head and say thank you.
